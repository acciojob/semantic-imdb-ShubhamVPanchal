Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller where Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, steals secrets from within the subconscious. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for performing inception — planting an idea into someone's mind.

Actors

The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe. Each actor delivers a stunning performance, enhancing the film’s complexity and emotional depth.
